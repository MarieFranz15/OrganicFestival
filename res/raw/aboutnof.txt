
Negros Island Organic Farmers Festival showcases Negros Occidental and Negros Oriental organic farmers, innovation, and products.

Organic farmers, advocates, top chefs, restaurateurs and tourists from all over the country gather in this annual event to celebrate organic food, organic farming, and organic lifestyle in the island.

This year, we will be celebrating the 9th Negros Island Organic Farmers Festival on November 19-23, 2014.

Main venue of the festivities will be at the PNB Event Area (North Capitol Road, beside Capitol Lagoon), Bacolod City, Negros Occidental.

The theme for this year's celebration is "One Negros, One Green Economy"
